Yata

"Yata" stands for "Yet Another Todo App", It is made for a hackathon in about 14 days. It is a comprehensive todo list based on #hashtags. You can label todos, create deadline, edit todos and archive them.

TalkNow

A global chatroom made in just ~5 hours to chat with friends. You can embed an image, embed gifs with /[gifname]. It helped us chat and exchange information from one PC to another as there was no easier means of communication in college labs. It was fun making it and watch people using it.

HTML5 • SocketIO • Nodejs • Material Design • PWA
